General Living & Community Spaces

Naawi Oodena, located in the city Winnipeg, is a hub of activity, living, and commerce. The local Safeway offers residents a convenient place to shop for groceries, providing fresh produce, household essentials, and a friendly atmosphere. The store is known for its commitment to quality and community engagement, often organizing events or supporting local initiatives.

Housing

Each residence is crafted with a keen eye for detail, featuring state-of-the-art amenities and stylish interiors. Spacious living rooms, gourmet kitchens, and indulgent spa-like bathrooms characterize these homes, providing an oasis of comfort and sophistication.

Apartments

Naawi Oodena has beautiful apartments located inside the northern area of the community, these apartments feature beautiful sky views, and gorgeous locales near them which allow total recreation.

Recreation

In Naawi Oodena, recreation is a vital part of community life, with meticulously maintained parks, jogging trails, and recreational spaces fostering a healthy and active lifestyle. The beating heart of sports and camaraderie is the community's state-of-the-art hockey arena, where residents gather to cheer for local teams, enjoy thrilling matches, and celebrate the timeless passion for hockey that unites the community.

Cultural Campus & Education

Naawi Oodena education is a cornerstone of community life, with a focus on providing residents access to high-quality learning opportunities. The neighborhood is likely home to a variety of educational institutions that cater to students of all ages, creating a nurturing environment for academic growth and development.

Mixed-Use Village

Welcome to the heart of Naawi Oodena, where a visionary mixed-use village seamlessly blends residential, commercial, and recreational spaces, creating a dynamic and self-sustaining community. This innovative development represents a modern approach to urban planning, emphasizing convenience, sustainability, and a rich array of amenities for residents.
